# Translation Protocol & The Beautiful Chain

- **Invariant Roots**: Every trilateral root in the Qur'an has ONE invariant operational meaning. Every occurrence of that root should be traced back to that invariant function.
- **Names as Functions**: Names (Asmā') are not arbitrary labels but specific, operational functions, as taught to Adam (2:31).
- **Arabic Invariants**: Intentionally keep core nouns and concepts in their original Arabic form (e.g. Nār, Jannah, Māl) in the English translation, preserving their programmatic accuracy.
